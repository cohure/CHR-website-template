CHR 2024

Fifth Conference on
Computational Humanities Research

December 4-6, 2024

REGISTRATION IS NOW OPEN
(DEADLINE NOVEMBER 25, 2024)


The Computational Humanities Research (CHR) community is an international and interdisciplinary community that supports researchers with an interest in computational approaches to the humanities.

The 2024 edition of the Computational Humanities Research conference will take place on December 4-6, 2024 at Aarhus University, Denmark. Check out the Announcements for the latest conference updates, and get practical information about the location on the Venue page.
